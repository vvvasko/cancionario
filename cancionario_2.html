Optional: enable live AI translation

Cancionario always checks a large built-in Spanish→English dictionary first — that part works everywhere, including once this page is hosted on its own. For words the dictionary doesn't recognize, you can either translate them yourself with one tap, or paste your own Anthropic API key here so the app can look them up automatically. Your key stays in your browser only — it's never sent anywhere but Anthropic's API, and it's not saved anywhere by whoever shares this page with you.
